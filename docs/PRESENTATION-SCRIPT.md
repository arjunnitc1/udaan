# Udaan Presentation Script 🎤

A simple guide for what to say during your demo. Just follow along!

---

## 1. OPENING (30 seconds)

**Say this:**
> "Hi everyone! I'm [your name] and this is **Udaan** — which means 'flight' in Hindi.
>
> Udaan is an AI business coach that helps Indian women turn their existing skills into their first independent income.
>
> Let me show you how it works."

---

## 2. THE PROBLEM (45 seconds)

**Say this:**
> "Here's the problem we're solving:
>
> 300 million Indian women have skills — cooking, stitching, mehendi, teaching — but they've never earned money from them.
>
> Why? Three barriers:
> 1. **They don't know how to price** — they've always done it for free
> 2. **They don't know where to start** — no one taught them business
> 3. **They feel they need permission** — society hasn't told them they can
>
> Existing solutions don't work for them. YouTube is in English. Business courses cost money. And ChatGPT doesn't understand their context."

---

## 3. THE SOLUTION (30 seconds)

**Say this:**
> "Udaan is different. It's:
> - **Voice-first** — she can speak in Hindi, no typing needed
> - **Judgment-free** — the AI never questions if she should work
> - **Actionable** — in 3 questions, she gets a complete business kit
>
> Let me show you a demo."

---

## 4. LIVE DEMO (3-4 minutes)

### Option A: Show the offline demo (recommended - no API issues)

**Say this:**
> "Let me show you Meena's story. She does mehendi at family weddings for free."

**Do this:**
1. Open the app → Go to Coach page
2. Click "Watch demos (no API needed)"
3. Select "✨ Meena's Mehendi"
4. Let it play (or skip ahead)

**While it plays, say:**
> "Watch how the coach asks just 3 simple questions...
>
> Now look at this — in under 2 minutes, Meena has:
> - Three business ideas with income ranges
> - Exact pricing for her services
> - A WhatsApp message ready to send
> - Government schemes she qualifies for
> - A week-1 action plan
>
> She went from 'I do free mehendi' to '₹15,000/month business plan' in 2 minutes."

### Option B: If you have API working

**Say this:**
> "Let me show you a live conversation."

**Do this:**
1. Click the microphone and say: "I make good food, everyone loves my achar"
2. Let the AI respond
3. Answer 2-3 questions
4. Show the generated kit

---

## 5. KEY FEATURES (1 minute)

**Say this:**
> "Let me quickly show you what else Udaan offers:
>
> **Finance Tracker** — [click to Finance page] — she can track income and expenses, even by voice
>
> **Goals** — [click to Goals page] — she can set savings goals and track progress
>
> **Piggy Bank** — [click to Piggy Bank page] — separate jars for separate dreams
>
> **Community Heroes** — [click to Community page] — real women who've done it, she can WhatsApp them directly
>
> Everything is in 4 languages — English, Hindi, Bengali, Malayalam."

**Do this:** Click the language selector and switch to Hindi briefly to show it works.

---

## 6. WHY IT'S NOT JUST A CHATBOT (1 minute)

**Say this:**
> "Now, you might think — isn't this just a ChatGPT wrapper? No. Here's why:
>
> **1. Curated Scheme Database** — We have real government schemes with eligibility rules. The AI can only recommend schemes she actually qualifies for. No hallucination.
>
> **2. Deterministic Pricing** — The pricing logic is in code, not the AI. When we say ₹80 per tiffin, it's calculated from real costs, not guessed.
>
> **3. Behavioral Design** — The prompts are designed by understanding how Indian women think. The AI never questions her right to work. It counts her labor as a cost. These aren't features — they're values baked into the system.
>
> **4. Works Offline** — The demos work without any API. Voice works through the browser. She can explore the full app even with bad internet."

---

**Say this:**

> - **Target:** 300 million Indian women with untapped skills
> - **Distribution:** Through SHGs, ASHA workers, NGOs — trusted community channels
> - **North star metric:** First sale within 7 days of using Udaan
>
> If we help just 0.1% of these women earn ₹10,000/month extra, that's ₹36,000 crore added to the Indian economy annually."

**Say this:**

>
> Udaan takes a woman who says 'I just cook for my family' and helps her say 'I run a tiffin business.'
>
> It's voice-first, judgment-free, and built for the women who need it most.

## HANDLING Q&A

### "How is this different from ChatGPT?"
> "Three ways: First, ChatGPT doesn't know Indian government schemes — we have a curated database. Second, ChatGPT might say 'ask your husband' — our AI is designed to never do that. Third, ChatGPT needs internet and typing — our demos work offline with voice."

### "How will you reach these women?"
> "Through SHGs — Self Help Groups. There are 9 million SHGs in India with 100 million women. They already meet weekly. One phone per group is enough. We also partner with ASHA workers and NGOs."

### "How do you make money?"
> "Three ways: Premium features for power users, B2B partnerships with microfinance institutions who want to support their borrowers, and government partnerships for skill development programs."

### "What's your tech stack?"
> "Next.js 14, TypeScript, Claude API for coaching, Web Speech API for voice. Works on any phone browser — no app download needed."

### "What if she doesn't have a smartphone?"
> "SHG model — one phone per group of 10-15 women. They share. Also, basic features work on any phone with a browser."

### "How do you handle languages?"
> "Currently 4 languages — English, Hindi, Bengali, Malayalam. The AI understands code-switching (mixing languages). Voice works in Hindi through browser's speech recognition."

---

## QUICK TIPS

1. **Speak slowly** — Judges need to process
2. **Point at the screen** — Show what you're talking about
3. **Use the demos** — They're designed to impress
4. **Say "didi"** — It shows you understand the audience (didi = elder sister in Hindi)
5. **Show the WhatsApp message** — It's the most tangible output
6. **Switch languages** — It's a wow moment
